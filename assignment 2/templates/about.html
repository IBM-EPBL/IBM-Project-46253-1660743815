Graduated students from various fields have wide employment opportunities. Each job in various field have different skill requirement for the interest of work field . Therefore, skill classification is needed to find out suitable career recommendation. Data from different students by accessing their profile using k-means clustering algorithm to find out hoe feasible the graduate for that particular job.But for the concern to safety purpose some of the details which are considered to be sensitive are hidden using text and images steganography algorithm to avoid misuse of data by attackers. After the assent from the students, that particular job provider can access the hidden-details, then collaboration filtering method by K.NN algorithm used to determine the classification based on proximity between, jobs and students skills.
